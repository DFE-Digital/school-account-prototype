Get school business information is a trial service currently open to school business professionals at some schools and trusts.

Your institution is not yet included in the service, but more are being added all the time.

Find out when your institution will be added to the service.

Some institutions, for example any that are not schools or trusts, will not be part of the service. These include:

  • independent schools
  • international schools
  • hospital schools
  • pupil referral units
  • universities
  • nurseries
  • training providers
  • local authorities

About the service

Get school business information lets school business professionals see relevant tasks and deadlines.

The trial service will be added to in coming months. To begin with, users who are part of the trial can:

  • see mandatory financial tasks for the DfE
  • see what funds are available
  • get DfE school finance-related updates
  • access DfE guidance and tools to help you manage your finances
  • find out what events are happening, including webinars and dial-in Q&A sessions
