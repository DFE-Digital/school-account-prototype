Additional payments for early career teachers

Additional payments are available to early career teachers.

You may be eligible for an early-career payment, if you qualified in and now teach chemistry, foreign languages, mathematics or physics in an eligible school.

You may be able to apply for a levelling up premium payment if you qualified in and now teach chemistry, computing, mathematics or physics in an eligible school.

If you are eligible for both payments, you will only be able to claim one of these payments this year.
