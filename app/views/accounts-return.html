Academy Accounts Return

Academy trusts and auditors need to complete an annual accounts return.

Check what you need to do to complete your accounts return.

  1. Step 1: Read the Academies Accounts Direction

    The Academies Accounts Direction is used by academy trusts, their auditors and reporting accountants to prepare and audit financial statements for the accounting period ending on 31 August annually.

    1. Read the Academies account direction

  2. Step 2: Consider automating your accounts returns

    The academies accounts return can be automated. You can electronically submit your trial balance data directly from your financial management software to the department, where it will be used to pre-populate up to 80% of your accounts return.

    Draft financial statements open for automated accounts on 1 September 2023.

    1. Automate your accounts returns

  3. Step 3: Learn how to automate your accounts return

    The DfE run regular free workshops, dial-in Q&A sessions and webinars to help academies find out how to automate the accounts return.

    1. View events

  4. Step 4: Prepare your submission

    The accounts return opens on 18 October 2023 so you can prepare an annual report and submission.

    1. Prepare accounts return

  5. Step 5: Submit audited accounts

    You will need to submit your audited accounts and an auditor’s regularity assurance report to ESFA by 31 December 2023.

  6. Step 6: Submit the accounts return

    You must pass your theory test before you can book your driving test.

    1. Submit your accounts return

  7. Step 7: Publish the audited accounts

    You will need to publish your audited accounts on your trust’s website by 31 January 2024.
