Academies accounts return

Deadline: 19 January 2024

Academy trusts and auditors need to complete an annual accounts return.

Read our guide below for all the stages of your academies accounts return.

  1. Step 1: Read about the academies accounts return

    Information for academy trusts to complete their accounts return.

    1. Read about academies accounts return (opens in new tab)

  2. Step 2: Use the chart of accounts

    Financial data structure and guide for automating academy trust financial returns.

    1. Use chart of accounts (opens in new tab)

  3. Step 3: Consider automating your accounts return

    The academies accounts return can be automated. You can electronically submit your trial balance data directly from your financial management software to the department, where it will be used to pre-populate up to 80% of your accounts return.

    Draft financial statements open for automated accounts on 1 September 2023.

    1. Automate your accounts return (opens in new tab)

  4. Step 4: Learn how to automate your accounts return

    The DfE run regular free workshops, dial-in Q&A sessions and webinars to help academies find out how to automate the accounts return.

    1. View events

  5. Step 5: Prepare your submission

    The accounts return opens on 18 October 2023 so you can prepare an annual report and submission.

    1. Prepare accounts return (opens in new tab)

  6. Step 6: Assign an auditor

    You will need to assign an auditor to audit your accounts. Make sure to give them all the permissions they need to be able to do this.

  7. Step 7: Submit the accounts return to the DfE

    You will need to submit the accounts return by 19 January 2024.

    1. Submit your accounts return (opens in new tab)
