Stay up to date cards ideation (Sprint 0)

This page shows ideation around improving the grouping and readability of information on the cards that appear within the 'Stay up to date' section.

Design 1

This is the design we have in the prototype at the end of the alpha stage.

Thoughts on this design:

  • On the 'Latest news and updates' card, the 'Published by' text is quite pale and lost among the blue links.
  • On the 'Mandatory tasks' card, the information about the upcoming task is dense and not very easy to read.
  • The current split between the cards (two-thirds and one-third) may suggest that mandatory tasks is less important than latest news.

Design 2

This idea shows the cards split into two 'half' columns to see whether this helps explain suggest that these items have more equal importance.

Thoughts on this design:

  • there is only one mandatory task so perhaps the mandatory tasks card looks a little empty compared to the latest news card.
  • it may be hard to distinguish between the two links on the mandatory tasks page

Design 4

On the 'Latest news and updates' card, the 'See all latest news' link has been removed and instead the link has been applied to the 'Latest news and updates' heading. Likewise, on the 'Mandatory tasks' card, the 'See all latest news' link has been removed and instead the link has been applied to the 'Mandatory tasks' heading.

Thoughts on this design:

  • the cards look very link-heavy
  • it may not be obvious to the user that the linked headings lead to 'all' pieces of latest news/mandatory tasks.

Design 6

The layout is a two-thirds column / a one-third column. The headings on the cards 'Latest news and updates' and 'Mandatory tasks' are unlinked. The 'Published on...' line is in a slightly smaller font size.

Thoughts on this design:

  • the size of the 'Published' line draws less attention to this so it's easier to read the titles of news stories
  • not sure that the 'Upcoming tasks' heading makes the card easier to read
  • splitting the 'Deadline' and date makes the content longer in the card but not sure it is any easier to read.

Design 7

  • easier to read the mandatory tasks content in a one-half card
